How It Works

Getting started is easy

1

Verify Student Status

Sign up with your university email and student ID. Admin verifies your credentials for safety.

2

Request a Ride

Enter your pickup location and choose campus or metro station as destination.

3

Get Matched

Our system finds the nearest verified student driver. Track them on the map and chat in real-time.

4

Enjoy Your Ride

Travel safely with a fellow student. Rate your driver and build community trust.
